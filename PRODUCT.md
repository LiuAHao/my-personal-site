# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, open-source collaborators, and peers in the tech community evaluating software engineering candidates or looking for project inspiration.

## Product Purpose
To showcase LiuAHao's engineering capabilities, specifically highlighting expertise in AI Agents, C++ Backend Systems, and Quantitative Strategies. It serves as a modern digital portfolio and central hub for their open-source contributions.

## Brand Personality
Professional, highly competent, modern, analytical, and curious.

## Anti-references
- Traditional generic resume templates.
- Overly flashy or noisy "cyberpunk" themes that distract from the content (the current neon/glassmorphism design is being moved away from).
- Cluttered layouts with overwhelming amounts of text.

## Design Principles
- **Code speaks loudest:** The design should frame the technical achievements without overshadowing them.
- **Structured elegance:** Information architecture must be clean, deliberate, and easy to parse.
- **Engineered precision:** Interactions and layouts should feel snappy, robust, and highly performant, reflecting backend and C++ expertise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast ratios for readability, support standard keyboard navigation, and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
